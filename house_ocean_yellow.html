Hands on Healing

Welcome to Hands on Healing, the premier massage therapy and acupuncture clinic and spa. Our mission is to promote symptom relief, relaxation, and overall health and wellness through massage therapy, acupuncture and a variety of other healing modalities. Our staff of certified massage therapists, acupuncturists, and other professionals are dedicated to providing the highest quality of care, and are committed to helping you reach a greater level of relaxation and health.

Our Services

  • Massage therapy: Our massage therapists specialize in different massage modalities such as Swedish, deep tissue, trigger point, lymphatic drainage and more. We offer a variety of treatments designed to relax your body, improve circulation, and help reduce muscle tension and stress.
  • Acupuncture: Our accredited acupuncturists can help restore your body's natural balance and improve your overall health. We offer a variety of acupuncture treatments tailored to your specific needs.
  • Spa Services: Our spa services include a variety of therapeutic treatments, such as exfoliation, body wraps, and facials. We also offer a range of relaxation treatments, such as aromatherapy, massage, and reflexology. We also offer packages for couples, bridal parties, and corporate events.

Our Facilities

Our clinic and spa is located in a serene, tranquil environment. Our facility is designed to provide a comfortable and relaxing atmosphere in which our clients can receive the highest quality of care. We offer a variety of amenities including complimentary tea and coffee, private changing rooms, heated massage tables, and private treatment rooms.
