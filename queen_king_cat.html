Hands on Healing

What is Hands-on Healing?

Hands-on healing, also known as energy healing, is a holistic practice that uses the power of energy to promote balance and well-being in the body. The practice involves a practitioner channeling healing energy, such as Reiki, through their hands and into the patient. This energy helps to realign the body’s energy centers and promote healing.

The Benefits of Hands-on Healing

Hands-on healing is an effective method for treating a wide range of physical and emotional ailments. While the exact mechanism of action is still not completely understood, hands-on healing has been known to produce the following benefits:

  • Reduction of pain
  • Emotional balance
  • Increased energy
  • Stress reduction
  • Improved sleep
  • Accelerated healing

Types of Hands-on Healing

There are many different types of hands-on healing practices, each with its own unique approach. Some of the most popular types of hands-on healing include:

  • Reiki – an energy healing practice originated in Japan that involves the practitioner using their hands to channel universal life energy into the patient.
  • Acupuncture – an ancient Chinese practice that involves the insertion of thin needles into the patient’s body to activate and realign the body’s energy centers.
  • Chiropractic – a type of hands-on healing that focuses on the spine and its alignment to optimize the body’s natural healing mechanisms.
  • Crystal healing – a type of hands-on healing that uses crystals and other gemstones to realign the body’s energy centers.
  • Therapeutic touch – a type of hands-on healing that uses gentle movements and specific techniques to promote relaxation and wellbeing.

Preparing for a Hands-on Healing Session

Before beginning a hands-on healing session, it is important to find a practitioner that you feel comfortable with. It is also important to inform the practitioner of any health concerns or conditions you may have so that they can tailor the session accordingly. It is also important to set an intention for the hands-on healing session, and to let go of any expectations. During the hands-on healing session, it is important to focus on your breath and take the time to relax and be present in the moment.

After a Hands-on Healing Session

After a hands-on healing session, it is important to take the time to rest and relax. It is also important to drink plenty of water to help flush out toxins from the body. During the following days, it is important to practice self-care and pay attention to any changes or sensations that may be experienced. It is also important to follow any advice or guidance given by your practitioner.
